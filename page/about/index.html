About Me

I’m Pedro Gomes, a 26 year old Software Engineer with a Master’s degree (in Software Engineering) who enjoys programming and technology. This is my webspace with information about me and a few projects I’ve worked on.

I have completed my studies at ISEP (Instituto Superior de Engenharia do Porto) in Porto - Portugal, where I also currently live.

During my college education I worked professionally for approximately two years with a development stack consisting of Angular, .NET Core, SQL Server, Azure DevOps and occasionally Ionic for mobile.

Computer Graphics is a field of great interest to me. Being able to visualize how math behaves is fascinating, especially when you’re the one writing the code. With that said, most of my personal projects are within this field, where I use graphics APIs such as Vulkan and OpenGL and underlying technologies that come along.

Nowadays most of my time is spent working with Flutter. A framework capable of developing high performance mobile applications for both Android and iOS as well as for the Web and Desktop (Windows, macOS and Linux). My master’s dissertation project was a mobile application for the Gig Economy that proposes a new communication approach between workers and their customers, and it was build with Flutter. I always try to develop having scalability as a priority and usually resort to the Flutter ecosystem to use reliable code capable of speeding up development as necessities arise.

Aside from programming, if I had to iterate through other things I enjoy doing, sports would definitely appear first. If you want to go for a long hike, swim, or run, and need a partner, give me a call, the more adventurous the offer the faster I accept.
